About Me

I'm a senior data scientist working at Optum, a business platform of United Health Group.

A data scientist does five things in this order of priority. I primarily do number two, but am capable to do any of them from scratch:

  1. A/B Testing on websites.
  2. Work with lists of millions of entities to place them into two groups: profitable or unprofitable.
  3. Ensure data is sampled randomly and recorded for use. Choosing samples based on ease, or based on decisions of yourself or the sample are discouraged, but sometimes acceptable.
  4. KPI Dashboards for sales funnels and customer retention.
  5. Give customer support website tools to solve customer issues.

I have done data science professionally for days.

My skills are machine learning, product analytics, data management, distributed computing, cloud computing, natural language processing, and deep learning. You can download my book for free here.

The best skills you can learn from my book are:

  1. How to have your code run fast and be organized. Without this knowledge, you'll likely have Spark programs that require 100-500GB and run extremely slowly. With this knowledge, you can sort millions of entities into profitable or unprofitable with only 500 MB of memory and running very fast.
  2. The 45 steps to follow to complete a machine learning project.
  3. Avoid overpaying by millions of dollars on cloud computing (In my experience, I've seen data science models cost $1 million a year, but rewrote it to run for free on existing resources, or could be run for around $50 a year otherwise).
  4. Suggestions on analytic database design, all components of A/B testing, and making website tools/dashboards.

I'm working on the Google Advanced Data Analytics Professional Certificate program and have my notes available here.

Also, I wrote a short ebook on the topic: An Effective Data Science Framework.

The methods I use often create models that are 10x more profitable than existing models, are easy to read, are easy to improve, and sometimes only take 1-3 days to create compared to the usual time of 1 year.
