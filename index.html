Summary

This page aims to teach you a trick to permanently remember 170 of Jesus' commandments. Click on the verse above to see other verses persuading you to learn Jesus' commandments well.

How to Use

1. Choose a room in your house you know well.
2. Pick a direction for the front of the room.
3. In this application, navigate to a location by clicking "Room".



4. Imagine that location in the room in your house, where #1 is the front of the room, #9 is the ceiling, and #10 is the floor.
5. Use your imagination and picture three objects that are related to the three commandments in that location.

The room button above will take you to one of the six rooms. Rooms store up to 30 commandments. It is recommended to start at room one and location one and move forward from there.

The randomize button is useful to quiz yourself once you are done. This can help fully ingrain this in your memory.
